My journey

Bethel Amadi is a visionary AI Entrepreneur, AI Product Architect, Product Designer, Product Engineer, Web Engineer, Web Developer, UX Architect, UI Engineer, Software Engineer, Front-End Web Developer, Front-End Engineer, and Design Systems Manager leveraging deep expertise in Artificial Intelligence to design and develop cutting-edge digital products. With a strong foundation in computer software applications, financial technology, and product development, Bethel has a passion for harnessing AI technologies to drive business transformation and innovation.

As the Co-founder and CEO of Klonix Technologies and CEO/Product Architect of Godfada Brand, Bethel Amadi has spearheaded numerous AI-powered initiatives, including solutions for financial inclusion, digital transformation, and intelligent product design. His background in Industrial Physics, coupled with certifications in Financial Risk Management, Cryptocurrency, Blockchain, and Product Design, provides a unique perspective on the intersection of technology and business.

Bethel Amadi's focus as an AI Entrepreneur extends to the development of transformative applications such as decentralized election platforms, remittance optimization, and digital identity solutions for financial services. His expertise in Generative AI, Machine Learning, and User-Centered Design has enabled him to create impactful products that address real-world challenges, particularly in Africa's emerging tech landscape.

A lifelong learner with training from AltSchool Africa (Diploma), the University of Cape Town Graduate School of Business (Executive), and Tech Global University (Master's), Bethel Amadi continues to push the boundaries of what's possible with AI in the fintech, mobility, and digital services industries.
